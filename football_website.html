THE GROWTH OF WOMEN’S FOOTBALL

Women’s football is the single biggest growth opportunity in football
today and it remains a top priority for FIFA. Although the game has grown exponentially

potential. FIFA is investing in dedicated funding, human resources
and innovative, tailor made development programmes, to bring women’s football into the
mainstream, where it belongs.
